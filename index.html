What is School of the Ancients?

A learning platform that blends AI, VR/AR, and primary sources to create mentor‑led lessons with historical figures. Our Operator loads worlds on command (“Load: Athenian Agora”) and AI mentors guide students using Socratic Q&A.

How it works

  1. Pick a world — Agora, Florentine workshop, early observatory.
  2. Choose your mentor — Socrates, Da Vinci, Galileo (more coming).
  3. Learn by doing — proofs, sketches, experiments, and debates.

FAQ

Is this open source?

Yes. Core repos live under the School‑of‑the‑Ancients org on GitHub. We welcome issues and PRs.

Do I need a VR headset?

No. VR is best, but desktop builds are planned. AR (XREAL, Vision Pro) is on the roadmap.

How do mentors stay accurate?

Mentors are grounded in primary texts and sources; we cite, summarize, and cross‑check. Expect updates as research improves.

Can creators add worlds?

Yes. A mod kit will let you add assets, scenes, behaviors, and lesson paths with a registry and templates.
